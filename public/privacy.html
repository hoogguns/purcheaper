Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 · Pilot operations — Wasatch Front, Utah

PurCheaper (“we”) provides device pickup logistics for online buyback partners. This policy describes how we handle information during the pilot.

Information we collect

  • Partners: company name, contact name, email, phone, website, order and device metadata you submit via dashboard or API.
  • Sellers (via partners): name, phone, email, pickup address, device details needed to complete a pickup.
  • Drivers: name, contact, vehicle, zone, job activity required to dispatch and verify pickups.
  • Leads: information you submit on contact forms.

How we use information

  • Dispatch pickups, verify devices, and enable same-day payment workflows.
  • Operate partner dashboards, APIs, and support.
  • Improve reliability, fraud controls, and coverage in the pilot market.

Sharing

We share data with drivers assigned to a job, the buyback partner that owns the order, and infrastructure vendors needed to run the service. We do not sell personal information.

Retention

Order and verification records are retained as needed for operations, disputes, and legal requirements. You may request deletion of lead data by contacting us.

Security

We use access controls (auth tokens / API keys) and limit staff access. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

Contact

Privacy questions: use the contact form on the home page or email the address you receive at pilot onboarding.

This pilot policy is a practical baseline, not formal legal advice. Replace with counsel-reviewed terms before broad production launch.
