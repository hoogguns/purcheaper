Buyback training checklist

  1. Confirm seller ID matches order contact.
  2. Power on device; note battery if critical.
  3. Check model, storage, color vs. app specs.
  4. Confirm no iCloud / FRP / carrier lock when required.
  5. Assess condition vs. quoted grade (cracks, ports, cameras).
  6. Photograph device + IMEI screen if present.
  7. Factory notes only — never factory reset unless partner SOP says so.
  8. Pack in provided kit: wrap, seal, label with order ref.
  9. Submit verification: match unlocks same-day pay for partner.
  10. Mismatch: still pack safely, document differences, do not promise pay.
