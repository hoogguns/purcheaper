  2. Choose PurCheaper — same-day home pickup on the Wasatch Front.

For buyback websites

Ops dashboard + API
  1. Push the order — dashboard or API when a purchase is created.
  2. We dispatch — trained gig drivers accept buyback orders in-zone.
  3. Verify & pack — condition, power, locks, IMEI vs. your expected specs.
  4. Pay with confidence — verified = same-day release; mismatch = hold.

FAQ

Dense answers. No fluff.

What geography do you cover right now?

Pilot market is the Wasatch Front: Ogden through Salt Lake City to Provo, Utah. Density first, then expand.

When does the seller get paid?

When the driver verifies the device matches the buyback site’s expected specs and marks it packed. Partners then release same-day payment from the dashboard.

What if the phone doesn’t match the quote?

Order is flagged mismatch. Payment is held. The buyback partner reviews photos/notes and decides next steps — no blind same-day payouts.

Do you use your own drivers?

Drivers complete PurCheaper buyback training before they can accept these orders. Capacity scales with our trained network, not a generic courier free-for-all.

Can buyback sites integrate via API?

Yes. Create orders with JWT or API key the moment a purchase is accepted online. Full status webhooks can be added in production.

Who packs the device?

The driver, on-site, to your packing checklist — not the seller guessing at a mailer kit.
