How PurCheaper works

Our couriers use a rigorous 8-point hardware diagnostic on your doorstep. If your device matches the quote, payment is released before they leave your driveway.

01

Get a quote

Your seller gets a buyback quote from your site as usual. PurCheaper adds same-day pickup as a delivery option.

02

Driver dispatched

A trained PurCheaper driver heads to the seller's address. Average dispatch time under 2 hours.

03

Doorstep verification

The driver runs a full spec check — IMEI, storage, condition, carrier — against your quoted specs.

04

Pack and seal

Device is packed to SOP with tamper-evident materials. Chain of custody is documented.

05

Same-day payment

When specs match: payment fires that day. When there's a mismatch: you review before any funds move.

What geography do you cover right now?

Pilot market is the Wasatch Front: Ogden through Salt Lake City to Provo, Utah. Density first, then expand.

When does the seller get paid?

When the driver verifies the device matches the buyback site's expected specs and marks it packed. Partners then release same-day payment from the dashboard.

What if the phone doesn't match the quote?

Order is flagged mismatch. Payment is held. The buyback partner reviews photos/notes and decides next steps — no blind same-day payouts.

Do you use Roadie / Shipt / your own drivers?

Yes — PurCheaper integrates with driver platforms (Roadie, Shipt, and others) and supports your own fleet via the driver portal.

Can buyback sites integrate via API?

Yes. Create orders with JWT or API key the moment a purchase is accepted online. Full status webhooks available in production.

Who packs the device?

The driver, on-site, to your packing checklist — not the seller guessing at a mailer kit.
