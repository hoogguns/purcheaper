Ops flow end-to-end

From your buyback cart to seller bank account — still inside one business day.

1

Quote won

Seller accepts online. Your system creates a DayLink order with expected_specs + payout amount.

2

Driver claim

A DayLink-trained gig driver accepts the buyback job and follows your packing SOP.

3

Doorstep verify

Power-on, locks, model/storage/condition checked. Packed to your rules.

4

Pay same day

Match → you release ACH/push pay. Device continues to your facility.
